Jaya Scholarship

Show Your Achievements and Seize the Opportunities! Every individual has the right to get a decent and quality education. For this reason, Sunbic University provides various full and partial scholarship programs for those of you who excel academically and non-academicly at national and international levels.

“The Jaya Scholarship for Outstanding Achievers” is one of Sunbic University’s flagship scholarship programs that allows you to study for an undergraduate degree at Sunbic University free of charge until the end of the study period. In addition, other scholarship programs are also provided based on achievement and test ranking results.
